## Overview

Enable shared team workspaces with role-based permissions so multiple users can collaborate on projects, prompts, and deployments safely.

## Problem Statement

Current collaboration is project-level and ad hoc, making permission management and ownership unclear for larger teams.

## Goals

1. Provide workspace-level collaboration boundaries.
2. Add clear roles for admin/editor/viewer access.
3. Improve enterprise readiness for multi-user teams.

## Requirements

- Workspace creation and member management.
- Role-based access controls across project actions.
- Invitation flow and audit trail for membership changes.
